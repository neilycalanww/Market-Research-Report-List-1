<p><p>The drilling fluid market consists of various types, primarily classified into Oil-Based Fluids (OBF), Water-Based Fluids (WBF), and others. Oil-Based Fluids are preferred for their superior lubricating properties and ability to stabilize wellbore, making them suitable for high-temperature and high-pressure environments. Water-Based Fluids are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and environmental friendliness, providing good cooling and cleaning capabilities. Other types include synthetic and foam-based fluids, which offer specialized properties for specific drilling conditions or environmental considerations.</p></p>
