<p><p>An Antilock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during sudden braking, enhancing vehicle control and reducing stopping distances. The market types are categorized based on the number of wheel sensors and control modules. </p><p>In a Four Leading market, all wheels have sensors, providing optimal braking across all surfaces. The Three Leading market typically features three sensor systems and one shared control, balancing performance and cost. The Two Leading market uses one sensor for each front wheel and a single rear sensor, optimizing cost efficiency. The One Leading market features a basic setup with one sensor for all wheels, suitable for simpler vehicle designs.</p></p>
