<p><strong>The Aerospace Touch Probes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Electronic Type</li><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Scanning Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace touch probes market includes various types tailored for precision measurement and inspection during manufacturing. Contact type probes physically touch the part surface, providing high accuracy. Electronic type probes use advanced sensors to detect touch without contact, enhancing sensitivity and speed. Infrared type probes rely on thermal signals for measurement, suitable for non-contact applications. Scanning type probes systematically examine surfaces by moving across them, allowing for comprehensive data collection and detailed profiling of complex geometries. Each type serves specialized needs in aerospace production.</p></p>
