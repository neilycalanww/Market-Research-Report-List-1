<p><p>The cheese concentrate market comprises two main types: powder and paste. Cheese powder is a dehydrated form, often used for convenient applications in snacks, sauces, and seasonings, offering a long shelf life and ease of transport. Cheese paste, on the other hand, retains more moisture and provides a creamy texture, ideal for spreads, dips, and gourmet recipes. Both forms allow for versatile use in various food products, enhancing flavor and richness while catering to different culinary needs.</p></p>
