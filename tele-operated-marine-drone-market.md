<p><p>The tele-operated marine drone market features two primary types: monohull and catamaran. Monohull drones are traditional single-hull vessels, offering simplicity and agility, making them suitable for various applications where maneuverability is essential. In contrast, catamaran drones, with their twin-hull design, provide enhanced stability and greater load capacity, making them ideal for tasks requiring prolonged operations on water and the transportation of heavier equipment. Both types cater to different operational needs in sectors like research, surveillance, and exploration.</p></p>
