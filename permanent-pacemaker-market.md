<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Permanent Pacemaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Dual-chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Three-chamber Pacemaker/Biventricular Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The permanent pacemaker market comprises several types based on their cardiac pacing capabilities. A single-chamber pacemaker typically stimulates either the right atrium or ventricle, primarily used for simple arrhythmias. A dual-chamber pacemaker coordinates pacing between the atrium and ventricle, enhancing heart function and rhythm. The three-chamber or biventricular pacemaker is designed for cardiac resynchronization therapy, improving the synchronization of heart chambers in patients with heart failure, thereby optimizing overall cardiac performance.</p></p>
