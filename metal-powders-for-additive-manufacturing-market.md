<p><p>The metal powders for additive manufacturing market is segmented into three main types: powder bed, blown powder, and others. Powder bed methods involve layers of metal powder being spread and fused, commonly used in technologies likeSelective Laser Sintering (SLS). Blown powder techniques utilize a stream of powder directed into a laser beam, often employed in Direct Energy Deposition (DED) processes. The "others" category encompasses alternative methods like binder jetting orMSP, catering to various specialized applications within the industry.</p></p>
