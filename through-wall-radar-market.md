<p><p>Through-wall radar market is segmented into 1D, 2D, and 3D types based on their dimensional capabilities. 1D radar primarily provides distance measurements by analyzing time delays of signals. 2D radar offers spatial imaging, allowing for the detection and visualization of objects behind walls in two dimensions. 3D radar enhances this further by delivering volumetric data, enabling users to comprehend the location and shape of objects in a three-dimensional space, ideal for applications in surveillance, security, and search operations.</p></p>
