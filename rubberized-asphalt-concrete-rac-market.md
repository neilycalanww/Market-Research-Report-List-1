The market primarily includes asphalt-rubber and terminal blend products. Asphalt-rubber involves blending crumb rubber with asphalt in a heated process, while terminal blend refers to pre-mixed rubber and asphalt at specialized facilities. Both types improve road performance, reduce environmental impact by utilizing waste tires, and contribute to sustainable infrastructure development, making them increasingly popular in modern paving applications.</p></p>
