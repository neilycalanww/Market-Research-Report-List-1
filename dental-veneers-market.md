<p><p>The dental veneers market primarily consists of two types: composite materials and dental porcelain. Composite veneers are made from a resin material that is easily molded and requires minimal tooth alteration, making them a cost-effective option. However, they may not be as durable as porcelain. On the other hand, dental porcelain veneers offer superior aesthetics and strength, closely resembling natural teeth while resisting stains, making them a popular choice for long-lasting cosmetic improvements. Both types cater to diverse dental needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
