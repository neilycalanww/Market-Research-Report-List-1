<p><ul><li> Continuous</li><li>Pulse</li><li>Scanning </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) laser doping equipment market is divided into three main types: continuous, pulse, and scanning. Continuous laser doping offers consistent energy application for uniform doping processes, enhancing production efficiency. Pulse laser doping utilizes short bursts of high-intensity laser energy, allowing precise doping control and minimizing thermal damage. Scanning laser doping involves the movement of the laser across the substrate, ensuring even coverage and flexibility in doping patterns, catering to various manufacturing needs in solar cell production.</p></p>
