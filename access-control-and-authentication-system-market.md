<p><p>The Access Control and Authentication System market is classified into Type I and Type II markets. Type I refers to the physical access control systems, focusing on securing tangible assets such as buildings and facilities through methods like key cards or biometrics. Type II encompasses logical access control systems, which protect digital assets, including networks and databases, often utilizing passwords, multi-factor authentication, or encryption. Both types work to ensure security but target different environments and asset categories.</p></p>
