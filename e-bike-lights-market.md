<p><p>The E-Bike Lights market comprises two primary types: front lights and rear lights. Front lights are designed to illuminate the road ahead, enhancing visibility for the rider and making them more visible to other road users. They often feature adjustable brightness levels and various beam patterns. Rear lights, on the other hand, focus on providing visibility from behind, signaling the rider’s presence to vehicles and pedestrians. Together, these lights are essential for safety during nighttime or low-light conditions.</p></p>
