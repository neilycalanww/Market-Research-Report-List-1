<p><p>Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUVs) are vital tools for marine exploration, surveillance, and underwater operations. The market is primarily divided into two types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered to a surface support vessel and operated by pilots, making them suitable for real-time control in complex tasks. In contrast, AUVs are self-powered and can navigate independently using onboard sensors and algorithms, enabling them to conduct pre-programmed missions over extended periods without real-time guidance.</p></p>
