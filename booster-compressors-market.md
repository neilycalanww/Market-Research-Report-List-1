Single-stage booster compressors efficiently compress gas in one cycle, ideal for low-pressure applications. Double-stage compressors enhance efficiency by compressing gas in two stages, suitable for moderate pressures. Multi-stage booster compressors further optimize performance by using multiple compression stages, enabling high-pressure applications and improved energy efficiency. Each type serves different industrial needs based on pressure requirements and operational efficiency.</p></p>
