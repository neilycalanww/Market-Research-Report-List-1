<p><strong>The Magnetic Track Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Track Brakes</li><li>Permanent Magnet Track Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic track brakes market consists of two primary types: electromagnetic track brakes and permanent magnet track brakes. Electromagnetic track brakes utilize electric current to generate a magnetic field, enabling controlled braking through the interaction with metal tracks. This type allows for dynamic braking with adjustable force. In contrast, permanent magnet track brakes rely on fixed magnets to create a magnetic force without the need for electricity, providing a simpler, maintenance-free solution. Both types enhance train safety and efficiency.</p></p>
