<p><p>The anti-reflection (AR) coatings market is segmented based on deposition techniques, including vacuum deposition, e-beam evaporation, sputtering, and others. Vacuum deposition involves the application of coatings in a controlled environment to enhance optical performance. E-beam evaporation utilizes high-energy beams to vaporize materials for precise layer formation. Sputtering employs energetic particles to displace atoms from a target material onto the substrate, forming thin films. Other methods may include chemical vapor deposition and sol-gel processes, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
